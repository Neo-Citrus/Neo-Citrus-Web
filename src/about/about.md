### [What is Frutiger Aero?](/about/frutiger-aero)

## More about me...

I'm an illustrator from the ***Canary Islands, Spain***, although I live in Sweden now (so glad I like the cold...)!

I created this website and these characters as a way to showcase a different art style that I don't normally do, even though I really enjoy this aesthetic. Another one of the reasons is because seeing a nice, sunny landscape when it's snowing outside is quite comforting, to be honest. 😆

I was actually a kid when this era was still on its golden days, and I especially remember the aesthetic because of the ***Wii***, and especially the ***Miis***, which you will hopefully see some fanart of at some point! Some would say I'm old as balls, but I swear I'm just 25! 😫

I'm also neurodivergent (***AuDHD baybeeee!***), and that's one of the reasons I want everyone to feel included in my art.

**My special interest are:**

- ***Gaming and game dev***
- ***Consoles*** and how they work hardware wise (I watch **A LOT** of restoration videos... 😅 )
- ***PC building***
- Disney cult classic movie turned hit from 1993, ***Hocus Pocus***
