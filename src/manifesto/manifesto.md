As an artist, I want to share my art and **bring joy to as many people as possible**, and especially **inspire others to make and share their own voice**, since that is what got me into art in the first place. I want everyone to create their own, unique, **human** art that **doesn’t rely on prompts written on a computer.** I want people to experience what it feels to make things with your own hands, **the pure ecstasy of seeing your ideas come to life.**

Part of the reason why I make art inspired by ***Frutiger Aero*** is because it brings back the little good memories I have from my childhood. My days playing *Super Mario Galaxy* on the *Wii* and *Animal Crossing* on the *DS* are the first that come to mind when I see this aesthetic, and the sunny summer days in my hometown while looking at the ocean on the horizon. This sense of **joy and nostalgia** for the past brings me forward to be hopeful that **this life can still be mine and yours, too.**

I want **everyone** to still have *hope* even in times of darkness and rely on each other. **There’s strength in numbers,** in community. Wherever you are, **you are not alone.** Rely on friends, family, your local clubs or communities. **There is always a place for you,** if you’re willing to listen and learn. There will always be a place for you in my art, as long as you make space for others to feel safe, too.

Keep up the great work! 💪😎✨

 – ***Neo*** 🍊💙✨
