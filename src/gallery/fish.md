Here's one of my very first Frutiger Aqua illustrations! 😍 

As I come from the Canary Islands, I have decided to make it my duty to showcase the local fauna (and possibly flora in the future!) to teach you guys a bit more about where I come from. And also it fits the aesthetic, so win-win! 😆

In this illustration, I have drawn a **Guelde** (*Thalassoma Pavo*, left) and a **Vieja** (*Sparisoma Cretense*, right). They're considered groundfish, and the meat from the Vieja is quite a delicacy! (I wouldn't know, I don't like fish. Ironic, right? 😆)

Feel free to download the pic and use it as your desktop wallpaper! It's my welcome gift to you all. 💙🐠✨
